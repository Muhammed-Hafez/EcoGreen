Who Eco Green ?

Established in 2018, EcoGreen is a leading company in aluminum and glass works. It provides comprehensive solutions in the fields of aluminum cladding, curtain walls, structural glazing, spider systems, aluminum doors and windows, canopies, glass roofs, mashrabiyas, and balustrades. The company has successfully completed many large-scale projects in Egypt, including hospitals, universities, hotels, and residential complexes.

Our Mission

To provide a complete range of services, including development, design, manufacturing, installation, and sales of contemporary aluminum and glass systems for buildings.

Our Vision

To maintain the highest standards of quality and integrity, establishing ourselves as a leading provider in Egypt for complete engineering services for facades and offering ideal solutions to each client.

Our Goals

To offer a full suite of services, including development, design, manufacturing, installation, and sales of contemporary aluminum and glass systems for buildings.
